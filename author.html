More about the author

Cornelius Griep is currently conducting research at the Technical University of Berlin and working as a computer scientist and consultant on Latin America at the International Office of the Humboldt University Berlin. In his free time he directs ethnographic and documentary films and promotes intercultural dialogue, for example, by organizing study tours to Africa and Latin America.
